How It Works

The ProCert Certification Process

Our rigorous, transparent lab-testing process ensures only the highest quality courseware earns the ProCert seal.

01

Submit Your Courseware

Publishers submit their training content along with the target certification exam and delivery format. We accept text, web, video, computer-based, instructor-led, and self-study materials.

02

Lab Testing & Analysis

Our quality assurance engineers systematically test every piece of content against the official exam objectives, verifying coverage depth, accuracy, and instructional effectiveness.

03

Detailed Reporting

You receive a comprehensive quality report with exam objective coverage mapping, accuracy findings, and actionable recommendations for any areas needing improvement.

04

Earn the ProCert Seal

Courseware that meets our standards receives the ProCert Approved seal — recognized worldwide as the mark of quality-verified training content by learners, institutions, and employers.

About ProCert

Setting the Standard Since 2001

Based in Bountiful, Utah, ProCert has been the industry's trusted independent quality assurance lab for technical training and courseware for over two decades.

We partner directly with the world's leading technology vendors — Microsoft, CompTIA, Adobe, Certiport, and Cisco — to develop and apply rigorous quality standards to third-party training products. Our mission is simple: ensure that learners everywhere have access to training content that actually prepares them for success.

Every title that earns our seal has been systematically tested against official exam objectives by our team of QA engineers. We don't just check boxes — we evaluate instructional design, technical accuracy, coverage depth, and real-world applicability.
